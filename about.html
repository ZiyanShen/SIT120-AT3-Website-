MISSION

Children's Gallery connect children to promote fun and accessible early learning and child care programs to meet the needs of children.

VISION

Children's Gallery aims to provide an inclusive haven for children. Our goal is to create a stimulating early learning and child care atmosphere that nurtures each child's social/emotional, physical, and cognitive development. We also strive to an engaging and well-equipped learning environment.

OUR STORY

The Children's Gallery was founded by Cho Chang and Fleur Delacour in 2011. They traveled to India and visited an orphanage. Children were full of hope but lacked access to education and recreation. It was what inspired them to find a way to accommodate all youngsters who wish to study in a fun way.

We, at The Children's Gallery believe that every child is special. The gallery, which is supported by kind-hearted public figures and organizations, continues to promote generational change and invites individuals to volunteer and help us achieve our purpose.
